About Ambient Backdrop

This app was created by Kyle Betlach, an individual with a deep love and appreciation for instrumental music, whether it be ambient, acoustic, electronic or post-rock.

The "mood" playlists are entirely subjective, but in general they reflect the type of music Kyle seeks out when in need of calm and quiet, or mellow background noise.

This is the type of music Kyle relied on extensively when learning how to code and progressing through a coding bootcamp. The creation of this app is a sort of "thank you" to the music that kept his mind in the right space, as well as an opportunity to learn about HTML5 audio and video.

Hopefully this app can help you in a similar way! Thanks for stopping by and having a listen!
